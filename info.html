About the Game

A 4-digit number with unique digits is hidden, and your objective is to guess this number.

    When you make a guess:
  • If a digit from your guess is in the hidden number but not in its correct position, you receive a "-"
  • If a digit from your guess matches both the value and its position in the hidden number, you receive a "."
Note that you cannot type any duplicate numbers, any numbers with more or less than 4 digits, and any other character
